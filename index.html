About the Book

A Collection of Life's
Quietly Remarkable Moments

SEEN is more than a book — it is a mirror. A carefully curated gathering of Snapshots, Conversations, Experiences, Nuances and Episodes that capture what it truly means to be seen.

"In a world that rushes past everything, this book asks you to pause — and truly see."

Each page invites you into moments both ordinary and extraordinary — the kind you have lived but never quite found words for. The conversations you replayed, the experiences that shaped you, the nuances only you noticed. Until now.

Written with honesty and warmth by Kelly Kantumoya, SEEN is the book that will make you feel understood.

"Conversations that stayed. Experiences that changed you.
Nuances nobody else caught. Episodes you carry still." — From the pages of SEEN

Questions

Frequently Asked

When exactly is the book launch? +
The launch is confirmed for July 2026 — the exact date is being finalised. All pre-order holders will be notified by email as soon as the date is confirmed, well ahead of time.
Do I need to pay anything now? +
No payment is required at the time of reservation. This form simply secures your place in the queue. Payment details and instructions will be sent to your email after your reservation is confirmed.
How will I receive my copy? +
You can choose home delivery or pickup at the launch event. If you select home delivery, ensure your address is accurate in the form. Delivery logistics will be confirmed closer to the launch date.
Can I reserve multiple copies? +
Absolutely! You can reserve up to 5+ copies in a single order. If you're ordering for a group or as gifts, leave a note in the message field and we'll get in touch with the details.
What is SEEN about? +
SEEN: SCENE — Snapshots, Conversations, Experiences, Nuances & Episodes — is a deeply personal and relatable collection of writing by Kelly Kantumoya. It captures the quiet, powerful moments of everyday life: the kind that shape us, stay with us and make us feel seen.
I haven't received a confirmation — what do I do? +
Check your spam or junk folder first. If you still haven't received a confirmation within 48 hours, reach out to us at kellykantumoya18@gmail.com with your full name and we'll resolve it promptly.
Can I cancel my reservation? +
Yes, you can cancel your reservation before the launch date. Simply send an email to kellykantumoya18@gmail.com with your name and the email used to reserve and we'll take care of it.
